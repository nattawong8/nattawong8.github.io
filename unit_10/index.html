Frollo

Frollo is a personal finance and budgeting app that helps people to track, budget, save money and improve their financial skills The client wanted to map out the customer journey and improve the app’s UX so that it would work better for their different marketing channels.

Wallsync

With WallSync users can take pictures of hand written post-it notes. The app digitises and categorises the notes and syncs them with programs like Jira or Trello. Users liked the overall idea, but didn’t feel the app would be intuitive enough or save sufficient time.

NSWOEH

The Office of Environment and Heritage cares for and protects NSW’s natural environment, culture and heritage. The main objective of this project was to encourage landlords of 20,000 rental properties for low income earners to adopt solar panels by 2020.
